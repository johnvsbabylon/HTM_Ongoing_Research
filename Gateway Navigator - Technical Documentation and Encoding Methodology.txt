2.1 Binaural Beats

**Mechanism**: When two slightly different frequencies are presented separately to each ear (e.g., 200 Hz left, 207 Hz right), the brain perceives a third "phantom" frequency equal to the difference (7 Hz). This is the binaural beat.

**Neuroscience**: The frequency difference entrains brainwave activity. Different beat frequencies correspond to different mental states:

- **Delta (0.5-4 Hz)**: Deep sleep, unconscious processing
- **Theta (4-8 Hz)**: Deep meditation, creativity, Gateway Focus 10-12
- **Alpha (8-13 Hz)**: Relaxed awareness, light meditation
- **Beta (13-30 Hz)**: Active thinking, concentration
- **Gamma (30-100 Hz)**: Peak cognitive performance, heightened perception

**Gateway Navigator Implementation**:
- Base frequency: 200 Hz (both ears)
- Right ear offset: +7 Hz (207 Hz total)
- Resulting beat: 7 Hz (theta range)
- Target state: Deep meditative awareness (Focus 10-12 equivalent)

**Advantages**:

- Can be combined with binaural beats for enhanced effect
